Privacy Policy

Last updated: Nov 3, 2025

This page describes how this personal Pinterest automation tool handles data.

1. What this tool is

This is a personal tool I built for my own Pinterest business account to help me post my own content and affiliate links faster.

2. What data I provide to it

  • My own Pinterest access token (not shared publicly)
  • My own affiliate/product URLs (for example, Amazon Associates links)
  • Generated captions that include required disclosures

3. What the tool does not do

  • Does not collect data from other Pinterest users
  • Does not resell or share Pinterest data
  • Does not scrape Pinterest

4. Disclosures

Some links I publish are affiliate links. As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.

5. Contact

If Pinterest needs to review this integration or contact me about API usage, they can reach me via the email on my Pinterest business account.
